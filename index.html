Hello !


I'm currently a student at Stanford University, USA, pursuing Master of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering. At Stanford, my aim is to gain a lot of hands-on experience in Robotics and I plan to specialize in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence.

I completed my undergraduate studies at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India where I pursued Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering. I completed my Bachelor degree project with Prof. Kamal Kar at IIT Kanpur, studying a prototype that functioned similar to bi-VAD (Ventricular Assist Device). During summer of 2019, I worked with Prof. Dilip Kumar Pratihar at the Center for Excellence in Robotics, IIT Kharagpur, and developed an algorithm for real time detection and prediction of gait events. During summer of 2018, I interned at Euphotic Labs, Bengaluru, a startup working on developing a robotic kitchen and redesigned one of their automated cooking machines.

For a year between my undergraduate and graduate studies, I worked as a Software Engineer exploring the field of application development at Bajaj Finserv Limited, a leading Non Banking Financial Institution in India.
