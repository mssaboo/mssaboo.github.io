I am a senior year undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India, majoring in Mechanical Engineering. I am primarily interested in working in the domain of Robotics and Autonomous Systems. During my undergraduate days, I have thoroughly enjoyed working on various projects across Field Robotics, Rehabilitation Robotics and Automation. <br/>
Currently, I am working on my bachelor's degree project with Prof. Kamal Kar on improvising the design and functionality of a bi-VAD (Ventricular Assist Device). In summer 2019, I worked with Prof. Dilip Kumar Pratihar at the Center for Excellence in Robotics, IIT Kharagpur, and developed an algorithm for real time detection and prediction of gait events. In summer 2018, I interned at Euphotic Labs, Bengaluru, a startup working on developing a robotic kitchen and redesigned one of their automated cooking machines. 

### Broad Research interests
* Field Robotics <br/>
* Autonomous Systems<br/>
* Sensing and Perception<br/>
